# JadeHandForward

J~ Natural Jade from the California Coast

## About

JadeHandForward is a live auction platform for natural nephrite jade pulled from the Big Sur coastline. Every piece is hunted by hand from the surf and stone beaches — never quarried.

## Features

- **J~ Jade-Coin System**: Tiered access system with coins that hold value across all auctions
- **Live Auction Events**: Real-time bidding on camera for authenticated Big Sur jade
- **Private Hunt Commissions**: Dedicated hunts for collectors who want stones found with their specific intention
- **Rose Glass Agent**: AI-powered chat interface to guide buyers through the process
- **Provenance Verified**: Every piece tracked from collection to delivery

## Tiers

- **$25**: J~ 1 coin · 1 pick
- **$50**: J~ 2 coins · 2 picks
- **$100**: J~ 4 coins · 4 picks (First Access)
- **$250**: J~ 10 coins (Collector tier)
- **$500-$1,000**: J~ 20-40 coins (Patron tier)

## Private Hunt

Commission a dedicated hunt for $500+. The collector goes to Big Sur with your intention in mind. Every piece found on that hunt is yours.

## Tech Stack

- Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript
- SVG coastal illustrations
- Google Fonts (Cormorant Garamond + Space Mono)
- Responsive design with ocean/jade color palette
